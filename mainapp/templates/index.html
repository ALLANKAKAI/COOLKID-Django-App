About Us:

Being “cool” is something most millennials identify with. It is primarily a raw expression of confidence with a goofy side. A cool person demonstrates endless energy and curiosity, and is always looking for the next big thing in fashion, technology and global trends at large. The purpose of Coolkid contest is thus to identify and celebrate the “cool” personalities within each of us and develop ways with which this energy can be harnessed for the betterment of the human race.

Who we are:

The Coolkid contest is a global movement that celebrates millennials and empowers them to make the world a better place through experiences that build confidence, promotes innovation and celebrates the “hustling” spirit.

Contestants:

The contestants and titleholders that have gone through the Coolkid contest are able to demonstrate passion and determination in their respective careers and use their talents and influence to advocate positive change in different socio-economic spheres.
